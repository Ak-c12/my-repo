Red Fort

The Red Fort, also known as Lal Qila, is a historic fort located in Delhi, India. Built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in the mid-17th century (1638-1648), it served as the main residence of the Mughal emperors for around 200 years. The fort is named for its massive enclosing walls of red sandstone and is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

Doll Museum

The Shankar's International Dolls Museum in Delhi houses a vast collection of over 6,500 dolls from around 85 countries. Established by K. Shankar Pillai in 1965, the museum showcases cultural diversity and craftsmanship through its exquisite, traditional, and contemporary dolls.

Airforce Museum

The Indian Air Force Museum in Delhi showcases the history and achievements of the Indian Air Force. It features a collection of vintage aircraft, weapons, uniforms, and memorabilia, highlighting India's aviation journey from World War II to modern-day aerial defense advancements.
